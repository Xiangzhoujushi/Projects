Xiangyang - My First Home


I was born in Xiangyang (/Hs'iang yang/) (襄阳), used to be called Xiang Fan，in Hubei province. I lived in Xiangyang for about 9 years. It holds my whole precious childhood memories. I really crave to live for some years in Xiangyang in future after completing my study.

XiangFan is an integration of two ancient cities: Xiangyang （襄阳） and Fancheng(樊城). The name was changed to Xiangyang, probably for attracting tourists. Xiangyang is a famous townthroughout the history because it is right on the "border line" that divides not only south and north, but also east and west. This "border line" is meaningful, because it partitions climates, landscapes, and even cultures. Moreover, its specific features also contribute to its importance in the war. It is the inevitable city to be conquered in all civil wars of through Chinese history.

It is also known among people because of Luo Guanzhong (罗贯中)'s novel, Romance of Three Kingdoms and Zhuge Kongming. (诸葛亮/诸葛孔明）The place, where Zhuge kongming lives when he farms, Crouching Dragon (卧龙岗), is located in Xiangyang. Now, it is famous because it is a main city in Louis Cha(金庸) ‘s novel, The Romance of the Condor Heroes (神雕侠侣).

Xiangyang is the "center" of China. From this beautiful section of Google map, every one can feel it (The location of Xiangyang).


Xiangyang is an awesome place to visit.
